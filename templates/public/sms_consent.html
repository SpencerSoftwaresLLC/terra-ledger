TerraLedger

SMS Notifications Opt-In

TerraLedger is a business management platform used by service companies to communicate with customers about active services and account-related updates.

Customers may choose to receive SMS messages related to:

  • job scheduling updates
  • appointment reminders
  • on-the-way notifications
  • job started / job completed alerts
  • invoice reminders and account notifications

How consent is collected

During customer intake or profile setup, the customer provides their mobile number and may optionally check a dedicated SMS consent box agreeing to receive text messages from TerraLedger-powered businesses. The checkbox is not preselected.

Message details

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Customers can opt out at any time by replying STOP and can reply HELP for assistance.
